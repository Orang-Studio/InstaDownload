InstaDownload lets you download Instagram videos directly to your phone without any server backend. Everything runs locally on your device — no external servers, no tracking, no ads.

Simply paste an Instagram post URL, tap Download, and the video is saved to your Downloads folder. Carousel posts (posts with multiple photos or videos) are fully supported.

Built with Chaquopy to run Instaloader (an open-source Python library) inside the APK, so no API keys or cloud services are needed.

Features:
• Paste any Instagram post URL to download the video
• Fully local — runs entirely on your device
• Supports carousel posts (multiple media items)
• Videos saved to your Downloads folder
• Light and dark theme support
• Share directly from Instagram to trigger a download

Note: Only download content you have the right to download. Respect Instagram's Terms of Service and only use this app for content you own or have permission to save.
